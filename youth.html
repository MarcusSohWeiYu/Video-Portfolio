Snippets Of The Storyboard

The storyboard’s primary goal was to pinpoint the core message we wanted the video to convey, focusing on youth leadership. We carefully crafted each scene to ensure that the emphasis remained on the empowerment of young leaders and the significance of their roles in society.

Played To An Audience Of 3,000

Project Overview

An exciting announcement video for the church, building anticipation and enthusiasm for the arrival of a dynamic Youth Pastor. This video unites the community, highlighting the upcoming visit in a way that energizes viewers.

My Role

As one of the video editors for this project, I was responsible for editing clips, sourcing the right archival footage, storyboarding and communicating with various team members, including animators, the data archive team, team lead, producers and directors.

Impact

This video was played during a church service at Heart of God Church to an audience of 3,000 globally, where it inspired adults to believe in young people and demonstrated to the youth that they have a seat at the table.
