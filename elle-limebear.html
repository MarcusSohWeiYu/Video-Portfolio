Video Announcement: Welcoming Youth Pastor Elle Limebear

Project Overview

An exciting announcement video for the church, building anticipation and enthusiasm for the arrival of a dynamic Youth Pastor. This video unites the community, highlighting the upcoming visit in a way that energizes viewers.

My Role

As the video editor for this project, I was responsible for editing clips, transitions of the video, pacing the voiceovers and soundtrack. I also needed communicate with various team members, including animators and producers, directors, to ensure a polished and cohesive final product that effectively conveyed the message.

Impact

The video successfully generated buzz within the church community, creating excitement and anticipation for the Youth Pastor’s arrival. It served to strengthen connections among members and foster a welcoming atmosphere.
