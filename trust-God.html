Why Do We Trust God?

Project Overview

Another chapter in the OA series, this video delivers a heartening message that encourages people to live life with purpose and intention. It’s crafted to inspire viewers to make impactful, fulfilling choices.

My Role

As the Team IC, I was responsible for ensuring that the video met high editing standards and followed best practices. This involved overseeing the editing process and collaborating with various team members, particularly in coordinating with the sound department to achieve a polished final product.

Impact

This videos was streamed live to an audience of 300 people, locally and globally to inspire people through Christian messages or through the Bible to live a more fulfilling life
